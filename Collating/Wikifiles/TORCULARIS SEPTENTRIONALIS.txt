'''Omicron Piscium''' ('''Omicron&nbsp;Psc''', '''ο&nbsp;Piscium''', '''ο&nbsp;Psc''') is a [[star]] in the [[constellation]] [[Pisces (constellation)|Pisces]], located about 142 [[light year]]s from [[Earth]].

It has an [[apparent magnitude]] of +4.26 and belongs to [[stellar classification|spectral type]] G8. 

==Name and etymology==
*The star has the traditional name '''Torcularis septentrionalis'''<ref name=rhalley>[http://penelope.uchicago.edu/Thayer/E/Gazetteer/Topics/astronomy/_Texts/secondary/ALLSTA/Pisces*.html Richard Hinckley Allen: Star Names — Their Lore and Meaning - Pisces, the Fishes]</ref>
*In [[Chinese language|Chinese]], {{lang|zh|右更}} ({{lang|zh-Latn|Yòu Gèng}}), meaning ''[[Bond (Chinese constellation)|Official in Charge of the Pasturing]]'', refers to an asterism consisting of refers to an asterism consisting of ο Piscium, [[Eta Piscium|η Piscium]], [[Rho Piscium|ρ Piscium]], [[Pi Piscium|π Piscium]] and [[104 Piscium]]. Consequently, ο Piscium itself is known as {{lang|zh|右更四}} ({{lang|zh-Latn|Yòu Gèng sì}}, {{lang-en|the Fourth Star of Official in Charge of the Pasturing}}.)<ref>{{zh icon}} [http://aeea.nmns.edu.tw/2006/0605/ap060519.html AEEA (Activities of Exhibition and Education in Astronomy) 天文教育資訊網 2006 年 5 月 19 日]</ref>
