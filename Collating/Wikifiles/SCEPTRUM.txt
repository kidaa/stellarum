'''53 Eridani''' (53 Eri) is a [[star]] in the [[constellation]] [[Eridanus (constellation)|Eridanus]].  It has the traditional name '''Sceptrum''' ([[Latin]] "scepter"). 53 Eridani was one of the brighter stars, designated "p Sceptri (Brandenburgici)", in the [[former constellations|obsolete constellation]] [[Sceptrum Brandenburgicum]].

53 Eridani belongs to the [[Stellar classification|spectral class]] K2IIIb and has [[apparent magnitude]] +3.87. It is approximately 109 [[light-year]]s from [[Earth]].
