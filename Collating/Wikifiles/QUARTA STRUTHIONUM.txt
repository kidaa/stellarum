'''Zeta Ceti''' (ζ Cet, ζ Ceti) is a [[star]] in the [[constellation]] [[Cetus]].  It has the traditional name '''Baten Kaitos''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]] ''batn qaytus'' '''بتن قيتوس''' - "belly of the sea monster").

In the catalogue of stars in the ''Calendarium'' of [[Al Achsasi al Mouakket]], this star was designated '''Rabah al Naamat''' ('''رابع ألنعامة''' - ''raabi3 al naʽāmāt''), which was translated into [[Latin]] as '''Quarta Struthionum''', meaning ''the fourth ostrich''.<ref>{{cite journal

[[Upsilon Ceti|υ Cet]] should be '''Khamis al Naamat''' or '''Quinta Struthionum''' (the fifth ostrich) consistently, but Al Achsasi Al Mouakket designated the title ''the fifth ostrich'' to [[Gamma Andromedae|γ Gam]] with uncleared consideration.</ref>

In [[Chinese language|Chinese]], {{lang|zh|天倉}} ({{lang|zh-Latn|Tiān Cāng}}), meaning ''[[Bond (Chinese constellation)|Square Celestial Granary]]'', refers to an asterism consisting of ζ Ceti, [[Iota Ceti|ι Ceti]], [[Theta Ceti|θ Ceti]], [[Eta Ceti|η Ceti]], [[Tau Ceti|τ Ceti]] and [[57 Ceti]].<ref>{{zh icon}} ''中國星座神話'', written by 陳久金. ==Structure==

Zeta Ceti is a yellowish star of [[stellar classification|spectral class]] K0 IIIBa0.1 and is approximately 260 [[light year]]s from the Earth. Its [[apparent magnitude]] is +3.9.
