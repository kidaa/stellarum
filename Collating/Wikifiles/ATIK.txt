'''Omicron Persei''' (ο Per) is a [[star]] in the [[constellation]] [[Perseus (constellation)|Perseus]]. It has the traditional name '''Atik''' (also '''Ati''', '''Al Atik''', Arabic for "the shoulder"). 

Atik is a [[spectroscopic binary|spectroscopic]] [[binary star]] consisting of a [[stellar classification|spectral type]] B1 giant and a type B3 dwarf orbiting each other every 4.5 [[day]]s.

==In fiction==
{{main|Stars and planetary systems in fiction#Omicron Persei (Al Atik)}}
Omicron Persei is perhaps best known from the animated television series ''[[Futurama]]'', where it is the solar system of the Omicronians- a race of giant horned toad creatures. Its eighth planet is ruled by the despotic leader, [[List of recurring characters in Futurama#Lrrr|Lrrr]], who loudly and frequently announces the name of his home system.

It has been featured in other [[science fiction]] including ''[[Star Trek]]'', ''[[Mystery Science Theater 3000]]'' and ''[[Transformers]]''.

==Named after Atik==
[[USS Atik (AK-101)|USS ''Atik'']] was a ship of the [[United States Navy]].

==Ambiguity==

Some sources, including [[Starry Night (planetarium software)]], an atlas,<ref>{{cite book|last=Mullaney|first=James, and Tirion, Wil|title=The Cambridge Double Star Atlas, Chart 7|year=2009|publisher=University Press, Cambridge|isbn=978-0-521-49343-7}}</ref>  and a web site<ref>[http://www.fourmilab.ch/yoursky/catalogues/starname.html Your Sky Object Catalogue: Named Stars]</ref> attribute the name Atik to the nearby, brighter star [[Zeta Persei]].
