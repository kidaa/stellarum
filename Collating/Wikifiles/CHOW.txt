According to the catalogue of stars in the ''Technical Memorandum 33-507 - A Reduced Star Catalog Containing 537 Named Stars'', ''al-Nasaq al-Sha'āmī'' or '''Nasak Shamiya''' were the title for three stars :β Ser as ''Nasak Shamiya I'', [[Gamma Serpentis|γ Ser]] as ''Nasak Shamiya II'', [[Gamma Herculis|γ Her]] as ''Nasak Shamiya III'' (exclude [[Beta Herculis|β Her]])<ref>[http://ntrs.nasa.gov/archive/nasa/casi.ntrs.nasa.gov/19720005197_1972005197.pdf Jack W. ==Properties==

Beta Serpentis is approximately 150 [[light years]] from [[Earth]] and is a member of the [[Ursa Major Moving Group]].

The primary component, '''Beta Serpentis A''', is a white [[stellar classification|A-type]] [[main sequence|main sequence dwarf]] with an [[apparent magnitude]] of +3.65.  It has two companions, the magnitude +9.9 '''B''', 31 [[arcsecond]]s distant, and the magnitude +10.7 '''C''', 201 [[arcsecond]]s away.
